For Whom We Do


E-COMMERCE MARKETING

Ecommerce marketing is the process of making sales by building and raising consciousness about an online store's product offerings and brand.

FINANCIAL MARKETING

Financial Market refers to a marketplace, where creation and trading of financial assets, such as shares, debentures, derivatives, currencies, etc.

TRANSACTIONAL MARKETING

Transactional marketing is a business strategy that focuses on point of sale transactions with emphasis on efficiency & volume.

TECHNICAL MARKETING

Technical marketing focuses on the key specifications, features of a product and is designed to appeal to people with technical appreciation of a product.

DIVERSITY MARKETING

Diversity or inclusion marketing is a marketing paradigm which sees marketing as a way to connect with the different individuals in the market.

RELATIONAL MARKETING

Relationship marketing focuses on customer loyalty & long-term customer engagement rather than short-term goals like customer acquisition.
